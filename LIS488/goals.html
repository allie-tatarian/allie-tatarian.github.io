Why Library and Information Science?

Hey! Your resume says your background is in biology. What are you doing in a library science program?

Great question! While working full time in a biological research lab, I realized that I was more interested in the dissemination of information than the creation of data. The world of scientific research and academia is called the "ivory tower" for a reason: primary research articles are dense are hard to read for the average layman (or even the average scientist in a different field!), and hidden behind prohibitive paywalls. I want to help people access information instead of sequestering away my work. The world of libraries and archives seems like a great place to do that!
