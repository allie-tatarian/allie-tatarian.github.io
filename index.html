I recently asked ChatGPT (the new GPT4 Omni model) to create me a personal webpage (including SEO and styling). I also asked Microsoft Copilot (creative mode) to write a "fun but professional" bio for myself. This is what they came up with:

Meet Allie Tatarian, the librarian extraordinaire who's turning the page to a new chapter of information accessibility. With a Master of Library and Information Science from Simmons University, Allie is not just a guardian of books but a crusader for open education.

At the Wolbach Library of Harvard University, Allie was instrumental in developing MetaSat, a project that's out of this world—literally. They contributed to the original vocabulary list and ensured that the resources were as accessible as possible, even to screen-reader and keyboard users. It's no wonder that under their watch, knowledge became more universal than ever!

Now at Tufts University's Hirsh Health Sciences Library, Allie is the go-to Research & Instruction and Data Librarian. They help researchers navigate the maze of Data Management Plans and share their work with the world. Before stepping into the library, Allie was a biomedical researcher studying both plant and human pathogens, which means they're as comfortable with data as they are with Dewey Decimals.

When not orchestrating the symphony of library science, Allie can be found in New Bedford, sharing a cozy space with their wife and a cat named Elton John. Whether it's setting up translation projects or orienting translators, Allie's work speaks volumes—sometimes in more languages than one!

So, if you're in need of a data wizard or a literary champion, look no further. Allie Tatarian is here to check out your queries and check in solutions!

I think this bio is cute! The tone is a little over the top, but it is in line with what I asked for. It's a bit out of date, and focuses more on projects that I've worked on in the past than what I'm working on now, which makes sense, because it only has access to information that is easily Google-able (Bing-able?). Everything it wrote is or was true, although some parts are not true anymore, and some pieces are bafflingly out of context (especially the line about "Whether its setting up translation projects or orienting translators..." Yes, that's something I did, but has nothing to do with anything in the rest of the bio).

For the record, the rest of the sections in this page were crafted by ChatGPT, too—I just updated the filler information it put together.
